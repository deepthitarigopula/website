EVOLUTION OF THE GROOMING INDUSTRY

In India, the professional haircare market was practically non-existent thirteen years ago. In 2011, the retail share of these products reached 25% via the salons.

It is incredible the way perspectives and interpretations of the term ‘beauty’ are transforming. With this, the trends and techniques are changing as well.

No more does age, gender, occasions, and skin tones restrict the definition of terms like beauty and grooming.

In fact, in today's world, the right grooming is considered as one of the important aspects that lead to confidence and ‘feel good factor. Beauty services have had a paradigm shift in their definitions. They are now viewed as self investments which leads to confidence and happiness. Also, irrespective of gender, today’s individual focuses on looking their best on a regular day at the office, unlike in the old times as the social constructs and conventional ways associated with appearance are fading away. Beauty and grooming are now a self-pampering experience to relish rather than an imposed condition of society to look good.
